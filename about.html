Our Story

From Workshop to Your Home

Bhisez Furniture was born in the coastal heart of Malvan — where master craftsmen have shaped wood for generations. What started as a small workshop in 2010 has grown into one of Sindhudurg's most trusted names in solid wood furniture.

Every piece we create begins with premium sheesham and teak timber, selected for grain, density, and longevity. Our karigar (artisans) — many of whom have been with us since day one — bring decades of woodworking tradition to every joint, every curve, and every finish.

We serve homes across Sindhudurg, Goa, and Ratnagiri. Walk into either of our showrooms and experience furniture that isn't just bought — it's inherited.

What We Stand For

Our Principles

Quality Without Compromise
We use only premium-grade solid wood — no particle boards, no shortcuts. Every joint is tested, every finish is hand-polished. Our furniture is built to outlast trends.
Made for Real Homes
We design for how families actually live — generous storage, durable surfaces, and dimensions suited to Indian spaces. Beautiful and deeply practical.
Artisan Legacy
We employ local karigar from the Konkan region, preserving woodworking traditions that date back generations. Each piece carries the mark of a master craftsman.
Custom Orders Welcome
Have a space with unusual dimensions or a specific vision? We love custom projects. Share your idea and our team will make it a reality, down to the last detail.
Delivery Across the Coast
We deliver across Sindhudurg, Goa, and Ratnagiri — carefully, on time, and with full installation support so your furniture is ready to use the same day.
WhatsApp-First Service
Get instant quotes, share room photos, and track your order — all via WhatsApp. We believe buying furniture should feel as comfortable as a conversation with a friend.

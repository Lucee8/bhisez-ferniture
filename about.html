Our Story

About Bhise'z Ferniture

Who We Are

Born in Malvan,
Built for Homes

Bhise'z Ferniture started with a simple belief — every family deserves beautiful, well-made furniture they can afford. Founded in Malvan on the Konkan coast, we've been crafting furniture that stands the test of time.

From traditional wooden mandirs to modern sofa sets, every piece is built by skilled craftsmen using quality materials. We take pride in our local roots and serve families across Sindhudurg, Goa, and Ratnagiri.

Today we have two showrooms — in Malvan and Sukalwad — where you can see our full collection in person and speak with our team about custom orders.

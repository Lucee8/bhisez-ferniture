To keep your Teak bed looking beautiful for years:

  • Dust regularly with a soft, dry cloth
  • For deeper cleaning, use a slightly damp cloth — always dry immediately
  • Avoid placing directly under sunlight or near heat sources
  • Do not use abrasive cleaners, bleach, or chemical solvents
  • Apply teak oil once every 6–12 months to maintain the natural lustre
  • Use felt pads under heavy objects placed on the bed frame
